# Third-party inspiration and licenses

The hybrid agent and skill-workshop design in Agents Pool is an original implementation for this Java/React codebase, inspired by concepts documented by these MIT-licensed projects:

- **Hermes Agent**, copyright Nous Research — persistent procedural skills, post-task skill proposals, isolated profiles and bounded delegation. Source: https://github.com/NousResearch/hermes-agent — license: https://github.com/NousResearch/hermes-agent/blob/main/LICENSE
- **OpenClaw**, copyright its contributors — isolated multi-agent workspaces, per-agent/shared skills, collaborator routing and review-before-apply skill workshop. Source: https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw — license: https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/main/LICENSE

No upstream source files are vendored in this repository. Official container images are referenced separately by `docker-compose.yml` and retain their upstream licenses and notices.
